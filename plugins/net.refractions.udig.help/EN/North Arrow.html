North Arrow¶

The North Arrow is available in two parts:

  • A new Map Graphic entry North Arrow that can be dropped onto your map; it will appear in the top left corner and will (gasp!) always point north
  • A new tool that can be used to position the north arrow on your map

This work is originally done with the help of a couple cool kids from Switzerland that did an internship at refractions research.

How to add a North Arrow¶

  1. Drag and Drop the North Arrow map graphic onto your map
  2. Change to the tool (right now it is a big red square because I have not made an icon yet)
  3. Click on the screen to position the north arrow
