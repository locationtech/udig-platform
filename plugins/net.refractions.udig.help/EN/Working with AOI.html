Working with AOIÂ¶

The concept of an area of interest is used through out the uDig application as a reference point to the area your are currently interested in. There are a number of ways to define the area of interest covered in the following tasks.

  • Configure table view to list the contents of a polygon
  • Configure table view to list the contents of the screen
  • Show the AOI on the map
  • Use a bookmark as the AOI
  • Use a Coordinate Reference System as the AOI
  • Use a processing region as the AOI
  • Use polygon as the AOI
